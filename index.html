The Sacred Ganesh Vandana

Invoking the Lord of Obstacles to clear your path toward spiritual clarity and material prosperity. A guide for your daily morning practice.

auto_awesome

Step 1: Purification

Before beginning, wash your hands and face. Sit in a comfortable cross-legged position facing East or North. Light a single Pure Ghee Diya to represent the inner light of consciousness.

"Om Gan Ganapataye Namo Namah. Shri Siddhivinayak Namo Namah..."

Step 2: The Offering

Hold a pinch of Kumkum and fresh flowers in your right hand. Close your eyes and visualize Ganesha's radiant form. Gently place the offering at the base of the idol or image.

Step 3: Sankalpa

The Sankalpa is your sacred intention. State clearly in your mind the obstacles you wish to overcome and the wisdom you seek. End the ritual by ringing a small brass bell to announce your devotion to the universe.
